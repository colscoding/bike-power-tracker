# Fitness Tracker Apps

## Advanced Training & Analytics Platforms

### [TrainingPeaks](https://www.trainingpeaks.com)
Advanced training platform popular with endurance athletes, offering detailed workout planning, performance analytics, and coach collaboration tools.

### [Runalyze](https://runalyze.com)
Free, open-source platform with deep analysis capabilities for runners, including VO2max estimates, race predictions, and detailed training load metrics.

### [Intervals.icu](https://intervals.icu)
Powerful analytics platform for cyclists and runners, offering extensive data visualization, fitness tracking, and workout planning with a generous free tier.

### [Golden Cheetah](https://www.goldencheetah.org)
Free, open-source desktop application for cyclists providing advanced performance analytics, modeling, and training metrics.

### [Final Surge](https://www.finalsurge.com)
Training log and coaching platform designed for runners, swimmers, and triathletes with workout planning and athlete-coach communication features.

## Mobile Activity Trackers

### [Cyclemeter](https://abvio.com) / [Runmeter](https://abvio.com)
Feature-rich iOS apps for tracking cycling and running activities with detailed maps, splits, and customizable voice announcements.

### [iSmoothRun](https://www.ismoothrun.com)
Advanced iOS running app with extensive customization options, interval training support, and integration with numerous platforms.

### [Strava](https://www.strava.com)
Popular social fitness network for runners and cyclists, featuring activity tracking, segment competitions, and a vibrant community.

## Structured Training Platforms

### [TrainerRoad](https://www.trainerroad.com)
Structured training platform focused on cycling and triathlon, offering science-based workout plans and indoor training integration.

### [Runna](https://www.runna.com)
AI-powered running coaching app providing personalized training plans that adapt to your progress and schedule.

### [Zwift](https://www.zwift.com)
Gamified indoor cycling and running platform combining virtual worlds with structured training and racing.

## Device Manufacturer Apps

### [Garmin Connect](https://connect.garmin.com)
Comprehensive platform for Garmin device users with activity tracking, health metrics, training plans, and social features.

### [Wahoo](https://www.wahoofitness.com)
Training app ecosystem for cyclists and runners, integrating with Wahoo devices for structured workouts and performance tracking.

### [Polar Flow](https://flow.polar.com)
Polar's training platform providing detailed fitness insights, recovery metrics, and personalized guidance for device users.

### [Coros](https://www.coros.com)
Training platform for Coros watch users offering detailed performance metrics, training load analysis, and route planning.

### [Suunto](https://www.suunto.com)
Outdoor sports platform with activity tracking, heatmaps, route planning, and integration with Suunto devices.

### [Fitbit](https://www.fitbit.com)
All-in-one health and fitness platform tracking activity, sleep, heart rate, and wellness metrics with social challenges.

## General Fitness Apps

### [MapMyRun](https://www.mapmyrun.com) / [MapMyRide](https://www.mapmyride.com)
Under Armour's fitness tracking apps offering route mapping, activity logging, and training insights for various activities.

### [Nike Run Club](https://www.nike.com/nrc-app)
Free running app with guided runs, adaptive training plans, and motivational audio coaching from Nike athletes.

### [MyFitnessPal](https://www.myfitnesspal.com)
Comprehensive health app focusing on nutrition tracking with exercise logging and integration with other fitness platforms.

## Premium Connected Fitness

### [Apple Fitness+](https://www.apple.com/apple-fitness-plus/)
Apple's subscription fitness service offering guided workouts that integrate with Apple Watch metrics.

### [Peloton](https://www.onepeloton.com)
Connected fitness platform featuring live and on-demand classes for cycling, running, strength training, and more.

## Beginner-Friendly & Specialized Apps

### [Couch to 5K](https://www.c25k.com)
Beginner-friendly running app designed to gradually build fitness from walking to running a 5K over several weeks.

### [Komoot](https://www.komoot.com)
Route planning and navigation app for cycling, hiking, and running with topographic maps and community recommendations.
